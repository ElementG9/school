ZERO POINT Banana Pancakes

My favorite recipe is ZERO POINT Banana Pancakes.

The ingredients are:

  • 2 Bananas
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Cinnamon

To make it:

  1. Put all ingredients in a food processor and blend well.
  2. Use a 1/8 cup for measuring batter on a griddle prepared with cooking spray.
  3. Cook pancakes 2-3 minutes on one side and flip – then cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl mix together sugar and cinnamon – you can also use the brown sugar blends.
  5. Add toppings to taste. Chocolate chips and whipped cream work well.
